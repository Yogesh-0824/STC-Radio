About UIET:

University Institute of Engineering & Technology (UIET)- a constituent autonomous institute of Kurukshetra University,Kurukshetra is situated in lush green campus of Kurukshetra University. The institute has been competitively selected as one of the 113 institutions in the country for MHRD TEQIP grant of Rs.10.00 Crores under scheme “Strengthening of Institute for improved learning outcomes and employability of graduates”. UIET has gained autonomous status from UGC under section 12F and 2B. University Institute of Engineering and Technology(UIET), Kurukshetra University, Kurukshetra was established in 2004 to develop a centre of academic excellence for quality engineering education in the state of Haryana. The institute is headed by Dr.C.C.Tripathi. The Institute is running four B.Tech degree programmes in the technically important Disciplines of Electronics & Communication Engineering. The institute is serving as a nodal agency for the matters of academic importance like development of scheme of examinations, Syllabus, academic plan and maintenance of standards in affiliated engineering colleges.The institute has separate building with all amenities and modern laboratories workshop, library classrooms and 6 seminar halls with in the university campus. Good infrastructure facilities are available in the institute like conference hall, labs, full AC reading hall in the library with more than 13,000 specialized books etc. UIET enjoys green lush university campus will equipped with auditorium, playground, library hostel facilities, Staff quarters and other facilities which is an added attraction for good quality faculty, technical staff and students.The Ultimate aim of UIET is to become role model for Engineering & Technology Education not only for state of Haryana but for the world over to meet the challenges of 21st Century.

About the Department:

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) was established in the year 2004. The department admires technology development through innovations and its exploitation for the benefit of human kinds. The ECE department at UIET is running B.Tech and M.Tech Programs in Electronics & Communication Engineering with intake of 120 and 20 respectively. The department has more than 450 undergraduate and postgraduate students. ECE department is also running P.hD with 8 numbers of research scholars working in various fields of Electronics/RF communication Engineering. Department is equipped with the latest experimental and computational facilities including Microstrip milling fabrication Machine(0.006mm), Photo-plotter (5mm line/space width), Vector Network Analyzer(10MHz-26.5GHz), High Vacuum Coating Unit(with E-beam thermal source), Quartz Crystal Monitor, Keithly Source Meter, Four Point Probe Station with digital Inspection Microscope, Auto lab Electro-chemical Impedance Spectroscopy for taking up R & D and consultancy activities in related fields. Various workshops on latest electronic design automation tools like HFSS, Multisim, Orcad, Xilinx, Labview, pSpice, Matlab to make students proficient in electronic design automation tools are being organized regularly. Besides the department is equipped with electronics instruments worth more than Rs. 2.00 crore and under the active guidance of faculty members, the UG/PG students have also been working on various microcontroller, embedded system based electronics projects.
